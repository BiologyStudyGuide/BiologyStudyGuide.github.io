Characterisivs of Life

NOTES: Carbs: 1:2:1 ratio (C:H:O) (monosaccharide & polysaccharide) also table sugar C12H22O11 is also a carb, but it does not follow the rule. Can be broken down for chemical energy. Short term storage. Monomer – monosaccharides. Polymer – polysaccharide. Lipids: Glycerol head and fatty acid tails (fatty acids and triglycerides). It is long term storage and stores more than carbohydrates. Polymer – triglyceride. Special type: phospholipid. Proteins: Carbon center connected to a hydrogen, carboxyl group (COOH), amino group(H2N), and R group (Amino acids and polypeptides). It has a lot of functions (enzyme, transport, signaling, etc.) Nucleic Acids: Phosphate group, carbon ring, nitrogen base (DNA, RNA, ATP) (Nucleotides)
